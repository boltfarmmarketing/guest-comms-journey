Step 0 · Acquisition

Two Ways In — One Journey Out

The booking channel differs; the moment a reservation is confirmed in the PMS, every guest merges onto the same automated comms flow below.

PATH A · Online Booking
Guest books online
Website / OTA — self-serve checkout, no human in the loop.
Instant payment captured
Reservation auto-created in PMS. No setter/closer involved.
PATH B · Phone Booking
Inbound / outbound inquiry
Guest calls or is called by a setter.
Setter qualifies → hands off to closer
Setter is the only role that hands a lead off. Assigned salesperson (closer) owns the guest thereafter — "if you talk to them, you own them."
Closer books stay & collects payment
Concierge-style preferences captured live on this call — the concierge step is fulfilled here. Guest flagged "phone booking / concierge handled." Commission pays on cash collected, not on booking.
Source: bolt:sops/guest-journey-and-sales.md — key principle & lead-handoff rule; commission-on-cash per reversal-index #346.
